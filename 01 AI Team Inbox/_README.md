# AI Team Inbox

Hand work to your AI team here. Drop a **file or a request** you want the agents to review or act on ("research this", "draft that", "summarise this PDF"). The orchestrator picks it up, routes it to the right teammate, delivers results to `02 AI Team Deliverables/`, and files anything keep-worthy into your vault.

- Want the team to handle something already in `00 Inbox/`? Just say so — the agent moves it here and processes it.
- **`00 Inbox/` vs here:** `00 Inbox/` is *your* stuff to handle yourself; **AI Team Inbox** is stuff you're explicitly handing to the agents.
