# 00 Inbox

Drop anything here, raw and unstructured — a stray thought, a forwarded email, a voice-note transcript, a photo of a business card, a link. Don't organize it; just get it out of your head.

**Role: the intake queue — transient.** This folder should trend toward **empty**: the AI triages each capture (extracts people/dates/actions, creates typed objects, links them) and archives the original. Nothing here is ever silently deleted. External/forwarded captures (email, files) always land here, even if you capture quick thoughts elsewhere.

*(If you chose `daily-note` capture, your quick thoughts go into today's note instead — that note is **durable** and stays as the day's record, unlike this queue. See `AGENTS.md` §4.)*

Set Obsidian's *default location for new notes* to this folder for one-tap capture (see `99 System/_system/obsidian-setup.md`).
